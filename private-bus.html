Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing & Services

What does the price include?

The price covers transport service five days a week, including both pickup from home to school and return trips.

Do you offer one-way or private transport?

Yes! You can choose a one-way service (either morning or afternoon) at half the full price. Private transport for a single child is also availableâ€”please contact us for a customized quote.

Are your vehicles licensed and insured?

Yes, all our minibuses are approved by the Land Transport Authority (LTA) and covered by Comprehensive Commercial Vehicle Insurance. Our drivers hold valid Bus Driver Vocational Licences (BDVL).

What types of vehicles do you use?

We operate 13-seater minibuses designed for safe and efficient school transportation.

Scheduling & Adjustments

How are pickup and drop-off times determined?

Schedules are based on your child's school timing and our route planning. Initial adjustments may be needed, but we maintain consistent timing thereafter.

What if my child is sick or absent?

Please inform us in advance if your child will not be taking the bus. While there are no refunds for missed rides, this helps us manage scheduling better.

What if school dismissal times change?

If there are occasional changes like early dismissal, let us know in advance, and we will adjust pickup accordingly.

What happens if my child is not at the pickup point on time?

If your child is not at the pickup point, we will reach out to the school directly and handle the situation accordingly. Our drivers are experienced in managing such cases smoothly.

Safety & Special Requests

What safety measures do you have in place?

All children are seated securely throughout the journey. Our vehicles are regularly maintained, and our experienced drivers undergo background checks.

Can I request a fixed driver for my child?

Yes, we can assign a dedicated driver to provide consistency in daily transport.

What if the school does not allow private bus pickups?

If private buses are not permitted inside, we can arrange pickup at a designated location nearby.

Enrollment & Booking

How far in advance do I need to enroll?

Your child can start as soon as the next day after payment. However, school entry permission for our bus takes around two weeks. Until then, we will pick up and drop off outside the school.

Can students living in Johor Bahru enroll in your private bus hire?

Yes, we provide cross-border transportation for students staying in Johor Bahru. We recommend subscribing to our monthly travel pass at $250 per month for a hassle-free commute.
